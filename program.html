building in America

Every international student arrives with a unique profile. We map the route that fits yours.

Pathway I

OPT & STEM Extension

Optional Practical Training allows you to gain U.S. work experience immediately after graduation. STEM graduates can extend up to three years — a critical window for building employer sponsorship relationships.

EB Green Card Routes
